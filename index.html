★ Award-winning · NYSC-commissioned

The CBT platform that started in a service year, and never stopped growing.

During my NYSC service year, I built a Computer-Based Testing platform to solve a real problem: schools needed a reliable way to run digital exams. My NYSC officials commissioned and recognised it with an award, and it's since become my major product, now implemented by schools running exams for students across Lagos and Ogun State.

  • Runs fully offline, no internet or mobile data required once it's set up on the lab's network
  • Handles both objective (multiple choice) and theory questions in the same exam
  • No printing needed, questions, answers and results all stay digital
  • Auto-grading with instant, transparent results for students and invigilators
  • Built for real exam-hall conditions: stable, fast and simple enough for any invigilator to run
  • Grew from an NYSC project into my flagship commercial product
